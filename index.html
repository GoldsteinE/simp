Simp

The simple image manipulation program

Download

Simp is a simple and fast GPU accelerated cross platform image manipulation program.

Full support for editing GIFS!

With Simp you can edit GIFS like they are any other image.

Cropping and resizing

Quickly and easily edit the size of images.

Perfect for pixel art!

Edit your pixel art without it becoming blurry.
